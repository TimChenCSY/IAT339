branding

Basic Branding

What does the company offer?

The company sells candy and other circus or carnival related snacks with a significant part of its identity focused on gift giving & shopping for other customers

Who is the company to potential customers?

As a "person" this company is sensory based, with a retro feel as well:

  • classy
  • nostalgic
  • dynamic/movement

Why might this company entice someone to purchase a product?

In comparison to traditional candy shops, which tend to either be very playful, or fancy and dignified, this brand aims to provide a balance of both. It would capture the simplistic ways of excitement of a person through candy and nostalgic carnival/circus environments. It also would lean into the consumer behaviours of buying for others; an important aspect of a consumer that pairs well with a candy business. This allows a customer to be able to fulfill gifts for a wide range of family members or friends.

Our brand is simple and classic, which emphasizes an association with nostalgic environments like vintage circuses and carnivals, both of which are associated with the product of candy. These influences are well known to be very distinct, vivid, memorable, and fun, leading to customers eager to visit or return again.

Font Combinations

The main font family used is Poiret One by Denis Masharov (2011). This font achieves a simple and classy format, while still having some playfulness. It can be found at:

Google Fonts

main heading

Main headings and sections heading are larger and all lowercase to place more emphasis on their playful form, rather than being more boxy and squared

Paragraphs are styled thin to lessen their importance, as much of the brand revolves around colour and images rather than larger passages of text.

navigation button

Navigation text is similar in style to paragraphs, but is larger and combined with hover & focus effects to ensure their importance differently from other text on the layout.

Types of Imagery

Our brand imagery builds upon the retro impression of the brand. The imagery is dynamic, and filled with colour, but also has a sense of unity without feeling too busy. One of either red or pink is a key colour choice to convey the overall theme. The carnival connotation of some of our images helps to generate a "sensory" feeling where the viewer can draw upon past memories of the vivid smells or sounds that the place is known for. Some inspiration examples of imagery options include:

A significant brand image that is represented closely with the brand name to convey our main business target purpose of gift giving.

Graphics with stripes or alternating colours builds our theme, and conveys it simply to the customer.

Vintage styles of images, graphics and hand drawn posters will feature on the main pages to emphasize retro branding.

Images in locations such as "about" which do not need to directly promote our product line convey the nostalgic, memorable, and warm feeling to the customers
